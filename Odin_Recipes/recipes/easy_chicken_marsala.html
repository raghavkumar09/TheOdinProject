Easy Chicken Marsala

Description

Ingridients

  • breast half, bone and skin removed (blank)s skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¼ cup chopped green onion
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • ⅓ cup Marsala wine
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • ⅛ cup milk

Directions Instructions Checklist

    Step 1

  1. chicken in a large skillet for 15 to 20 minutes, or until cooked through and juices run clear.

    2. Step 2

  2. green onion and mushrooms and saute until soft, then add Marsala wine and bring to a boil.

    3. Step 3

  3. for 2 to 4 minutes, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. Stir in cream and milk and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
